Our Mission

The National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) of Dergana is a higher education institution that trains high-level engineers in the field of advanced technologies.

Our mission is to prepare the next generation of engineers and researchers capable of meeting the technological challenges of the 21st century.

With laboratories equipped with the latest technologies and an excellent faculty, ENSTA offers a unique learning environment that combines theory and practice.
